## The Birth of Zentrix

In an ever-changing financial market, transaction costs have always been a major challenge for investors and institutions alike. Traditional rebate (backcom) models typically operate manually, rely heavily on human intervention, lack transparency, and frequently suffer from processing delays.

Zentrix was founded to break through these limitations. We did not enter the market as a traditional broker, but as a technology company (Fintech). By leveraging powerful server infrastructure and automated order-matching algorithms, Zentrix completely eliminates intermediary barriers, delivering a precisely optimized and fully closed-loop cost management process.

## What Sets Zentrix Apart?

Rather than competing through promises, Zentrix competes through core technological capability:

### 1. Full Automation

At Zentrix, the process of calculating volume and rebates involves no human intervention. The system is programmed to connect directly with the data gateways of partner platforms, processing millions of data points and distributing entitlements seamlessly and accurately.

### 2. Independent & Objective

Zentrix is a neutral technology entity. We do not hold assets on behalf of users, do not interfere with trading strategies, and have no conflicts of interest with our users. We simply provide a secure technological "pipeline" through which your rebate cash flow is optimized.

### 3. Data Transparency

Every status, rate, and technical parameter is digitized and displayed in real time on your Dashboard. What you see is exactly what the system is executing – no hidden fees, no ambiguity.
